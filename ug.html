Overview

Have you ever been walking in your city and you pass a street musician? More often than not, you'll only quickly glance and continue on your way, not really stopping to observe and listen. However, every once in awhile, you'll notice that one of those street musicians is insanely talented. What if you wanted to go back and find that same musician?

Currently, there's no central location to viewing and exploring the musicians of the New York Subway system. I got together with an old buddy, Nathan, who came up with the idea. We wanted to create a exploratory map of New York which allows anyone to check out and discover the talented artists of New York's music scene.

The project is still in it's infancy but we're pushing it forward every week. If you want to help us out, shoot me a quick note and we can see how we can work together.
