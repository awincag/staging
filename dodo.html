Dodo App

Dodo App.
Making mobile bug reporting less painful.
Challenge

Significantly reduce the effort and time to report mobile app bugs.

Solution

Working product and project management at a development agency, My partner and I knew that we had to remove the need to upload screenshots to your computer before making annotations.

The previous process included:

  • 1. Take a screenshot of the bugs
  • 2a. Plug phone into computer
  • 2b. Upload photos to google drive or dropbox using native apps
  • 3. Download / preview photo
  • 4. Take screenshot
  • 5. Annotate screenshot
  • 6. Open up project management tool (Slack, Jira, Trello, Pivotal Tracker)
  • 7. Create Ticket & add description
  • 8. Attach photo with annotations
  • 9. Complete

After about 16 rounds of iterations, we were able to get the flow down to less than half that. Our new flow included:

  • 1. Take a screenshot of the bugs
  • 2. Open Up Dodo
  • 3. Choose Photo
  • 4. Tap Bug Area to add comment (which is optional)
  • 5. Post to integration (Slack, Jira, Trello or Pivotal Tracker)
